Basic Math:
-----------

PEMDAS environment:
P - Parenthesis
E - Exponent
M - Multiplication
D - Division
A - Addition
S - Subtraction

The arthematic operationwill be performed from left to right allways even we give in the same line.

>>> print 2**8
256

>>> print 2**8 * 4 / 4 + 44 - 44
256

>>> print 100/2*3
150

>>> print 100/(2*3)
16

>>> print 100.0/(2*3)
16.666666667

>>> print 100/(2.0*3)
16.666666667

>>> print "100/16 is: ", 100/16, "Reminder", 100%16
100/16 is: 6 Reminder 4
